This Cookie Policy explains how **Letter Case Converter** uses cookies and similar technologies on [lettercaseconverter.org](https://lettercaseconverter.org).

## 1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. We also use local storage for consent and site preference state.

## 2. Cookie categories

### 2.1 Essential (always active)

Required for core functionality, including:

- page rendering and navigation behavior
- security and abuse prevention
- storing required technical preferences

### 2.2 Analytics (optional)

When enabled, analytics helps us understand aggregate usage trends and improve tool reliability and content quality.

### 2.3 Advertising (optional)

When enabled, Google AdSense may use cookies/identifiers for ad delivery and measurement.

Depending on your settings and legal context, ads may be personalized or non-personalized.

## 3. Consent and controls

You can:

- accept all optional cookies
- reject non-essential cookies
- customize analytics and advertising separately

Use the on-site **Cookie settings** control at any time.

## 4. US privacy controls and GPC

For US users, we provide an explicit opt-out path for sale/share style processing:

- [/do-not-sell-or-share](/do-not-sell-or-share)

When your browser sends a **Global Privacy Control (GPC)** signal, we treat it as an opt-out signal in our consent layer.

## 5. Ad and industry controls

- Google ad settings: <https://adssettings.google.com>
- Industry opt-out information: <https://www.aboutads.info>

## 6. Browser-level cookie controls

You can also block or delete cookies from your browser settings. Some site features may not work correctly if all cookies are blocked.

## 7. Updates

We may update this policy when cookie usage, tooling, or legal requirements change. The "Last updated" date reflects the latest revision.
