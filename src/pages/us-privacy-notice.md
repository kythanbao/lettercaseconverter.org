_Last updated_: February 22, 2026

This notice supplements our [Privacy Policy](/privacy) for residents of US states with comprehensive privacy laws (such as California and other applicable states).

## 1. Categories of personal information collected

Depending on use, we may collect:

- identifiers (for example, email when you contact us)
- internet/electronic activity data (pages viewed, broad usage events)
- device and browser data
- geolocation at coarse regional level
- support and privacy request information you provide

## 2. Purposes

We process this information to:

- provide and maintain the website
- secure systems and prevent abuse
- measure service quality and improve features
- deliver and measure advertising where allowed
- respond to legal and privacy requests

## 3. Categories of recipients

We may disclose relevant data to:

- hosting and infrastructure providers
- analytics providers
- advertising providers (including Google AdSense)
- security and anti-abuse service providers
- AI service providers (only for AI tool features)

## 4. Sale/share and targeted advertising opt-out

You can opt out using:

- [/do-not-sell-or-share](/do-not-sell-or-share)
- the on-site **Cookie settings** control

We also honor supported **Global Privacy Control (GPC)** signals in our consent layer.

## 5. Your rights

Where applicable, you may request to:

- know/access categories and specific pieces of personal information
- correct inaccurate personal information
- delete personal information (subject to legal exceptions)
- opt out of sale/share and targeted advertising
- limit use/disclosure of sensitive personal information (where applicable)
- appeal a denied request (where required)

## 6. How to exercise rights

Submit requests via:

- [/privacy-request](/privacy-request)
- Email: **contact@lettercaseconverter.org**

We may request verification details before completing a request.

## 7. Non-discrimination

We do not discriminate against users for exercising applicable privacy rights, except where a difference is reasonably related to the value provided by data under law.

## 8. Authorized agents

Where required by law, authorized agents may submit requests on behalf of users with proper authorization and verification.
