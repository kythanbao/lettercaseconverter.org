_Last updated_: February 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **Letter Case Converter** ("we", "us", "our") handles information when you use [lettercaseconverter.org](https://lettercaseconverter.org).

## 1. Who We Are

- Website: **Letter Case Converter**
- Contact email: **contact@lettercaseconverter.org**
- Service type: browser-based text, data, and image utility tools

## 2. Notice at Collection

We collect limited categories of data to operate the site safely and deliver features.

### 2.1 Categories of information

- Basic technical data: browser, device type, OS, approximate geolocation at region level
- Usage events: pages viewed, tool usage counts, broad engagement signals
- Consent and privacy preferences: cookie choices, Do Not Sell/Share status, Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal handling
- Support data you provide: request details you send through contact or privacy request channels
- AI tool request text: only when you use AI rewrite tools

### 2.2 Sources

- Directly from you (tool input, support message, privacy request)
- Automatically from browser/device during page usage
- From service providers that help host, secure, or analyze service usage

### 2.3 Business purposes

- Deliver, secure, and maintain the website
- Debug errors and improve reliability
- Measure product quality and content usefulness
- Deliver ads and ad measurement (when applicable)
- Respond to support and privacy requests

### 2.4 Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for the purposes above, legal compliance, and security/abuse prevention.

## 3. How Tools Process Input

### 3.1 Non-AI tools

Most tools process input in your browser. We do not require accounts for these tools.

### 3.2 AI tools

For AI rewrite features, submitted text is sent to our AI provider to generate output.

Do not submit sensitive legal, medical, financial, or confidential company information in AI tools.

## 4. Cookies, Analytics, and Ads

We use cookies/local storage for:

- essential site behavior and security
- optional analytics
- optional advertising (Google AdSense)

You can manage optional categories with the site **Cookie settings** control and at [/cookie-policy](/cookie-policy).

## 5. Advertising and US Privacy Choices

We use Google AdSense and related measurement systems.

### 5.1 Do Not Sell or Share

If you want to opt out of sale/share style processing for targeted advertising, use:

- [/do-not-sell-or-share](/do-not-sell-or-share)

### 5.2 Global Privacy Control (GPC)

When supported by your browser, we honor GPC signals as an opt-out signal for optional advertising and analytics processing in our consent layer.

### 5.3 Restricted Data Processing / Non-personalized handling

When ad consent is not granted (including GPC-based opt-out), we apply a restricted/non-personalized ad request mode in the site ad configuration.

### 5.4 Ad transparency

- `ads.txt` is published at [/ads.txt](/ads.txt)
- Ad settings info: <https://adssettings.google.com>

## 6. US State Privacy Rights

Depending on your US state, you may have rights to:

- know/access personal information categories and processing purposes
- request deletion (subject to legal exceptions)
- request correction of inaccurate personal information
- opt out of sale/share or targeted advertising
- limit use/disclosure of sensitive personal information (where applicable)
- appeal denial of a privacy request (where required)

For details, see our US-specific notice:

- [/us-privacy-notice](/us-privacy-notice)
- Spanish version: [/es/aviso-de-privacidad-us](/es/aviso-de-privacidad-us)

## 8. Third-Party Services

We rely on service providers for hosting, analytics, ad delivery, and AI processing. These providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

## 9. Children and COPPA

Our site is for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe a child submitted personal information, contact us so we can review and remove data where applicable.

## 10. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards. No transmission or storage system is guaranteed to be 100% secure.

## 11. International Data Transfers

If you access the site outside the operator's country, data may be processed in other jurisdictions where our providers operate.
